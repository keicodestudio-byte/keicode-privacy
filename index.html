Privacy Policy – BabyLog

Developer: keicodestudio

Last updated: 26 February 2026

1. Overview

BabyLog ("the App") helps parents and caregivers track baby activities such as feeding, sleep, diaper changes, and development. Your privacy is important to us.

This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data

The App does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address

2.2 Baby-related Data Entered by You

Any baby-related data you enter (for example: feeding logs, sleep, diapers, growth measurements, notes) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data.

3. Local Data Storage

The App stores information locally on your device for offline use. This information is not transmitted to our servers.

You can delete the stored data at any time by using in-app reset options (if available) or by uninstalling the App.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data automatically, such as:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • Interactions with ads (e.g., impressions and clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's policies. For details, see: Google Privacy Policy.

Ads help support the App and allow us to provide it for free.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

The only third-party service used is Google AdMob, which operates under its own privacy policy.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by parents and caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Baby-related information is entered by the user and stored locally on the device.

7. Data Security

Baby-related data is stored locally on your device. You control access to your device and can delete the App data at any time. We do not store your baby-related data on external servers.

8. Your Choices and Rights

You can:

  • Stop using the App at any time
  • Delete all App data by uninstalling the App
  • Reset/delete data using in-app options (if available)

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
